About

Born and raised in the Sardinian town of Quartu Sant'Elena a reasonable amount of years ago, I lived in Trento, Hamburg, Amsterdam and I spent some time in Rome.

I have always been interested in photography but I started taking pictures only after moving to Hamburg because it's too hard to make new friends when you are older than 25 and I had to find something that I could do alone.

I have a PhD in theoretical physics and a violin diploma, and this is the proof that I like to invest a lot of effort and time in useless things. This website is one of them.

I love sports, specialty coffee, niche perfumery, flags and many other things.

Currently based nowhere.
